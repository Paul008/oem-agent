<objective>
## Goal
Fix parser misclassification for text-only grids and add dark-bg testimonial style. Ensure captured OEM sections render correctly without needing right-click type override.

## Purpose
The parser currently misclassifies text grids (Overview/Warranty/Tech) as feature-cards because they have repeating children with headings. And the testimonial renderer doesn't support the dark-bg large-quote style used by GWM reviews. These are the two most common capture failures.

## Output
- Fixed section-parser.ts — text-only grids → content-block, not feature-cards
- Updated SectionTestimonial.vue — dark-bg large-quote layout option
- Updated SectionContentBlock.vue — multi-column grid layout
- Updated SectionProperties.vue — layout/style controls for new variants
</objective>
